Spatio-TEmporal INtelligence LABoratory (STEINLAB) was estalished in January 2022 as part of the Big Data Research Center at the School of Software Technology, Zhejiang University. Our lab focuses on exploring how advanced artificial intelligence techniques can benefit various spatio-temporal tasks in the fields of computer graphics, computer vision, and industrial design. Our research areas include urban modeling, remote sensing analysis, time-series analysis, human-computer interaction, UI design, and product design.

Our lab is currently funded by the Natural Science Foundations and Key Research and Development Projects at both national and provincial levels. We are currently seeking candidates who are interested in pursuing D.Eng. / M.Eng. programs at the School of Software Technology, Zhejiang University. We welcome individuals who are creative, highly motivated, and have a strong passion for the aforementioned research areas to reach out to us.
